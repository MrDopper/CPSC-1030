About Us

      My name is Jackson Vi, and I am the owner of this fan page website. This site is dedicated primarily to Queen, one of the greatest rock bands of the 20th century. They are legendary in the world of rock music, with numerous iconic songs such as "Love of My Life" and "We Will Rock You." Sadly, Freddie Mercury, the band’s frontman, passed away from HIV on November 24, 1991. Before his passing, he performed his final show, titled "A Night of Summer Magic," which took place at Knebworth House in Hertfordshire on August 9, 1986. The HIV/AIDS charity established after Freddie’s death in 1991 has since supported over 800 projects worldwide and raised over $16 million in the fight against the disease.
